Information about the coins in the jar

Before the trading period starts, some traders will receive information about the amount of some coin values in the jar; for example, the number of all 50 cent coins in the jar. There is a total of four information levels (I0, I1, I2, I3). Out of the participants, a quarter will receive the same information. Depending on your information level, you will learn the number of none, two, or three types of coins contained in the jar. You will also learn the value of these coins. A participant with information level I0 learns nothing about the number of coins; I1 learns the number and value of the €1 and 5 cent coins; I2 the number and value of the €2 and 2 cent coins, and I3 the number and value of the 50, 20, and 10 cent coins. Nobody learns the number or value of the 1 cent coins. The information structure looks as follows:
